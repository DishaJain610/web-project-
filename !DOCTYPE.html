How to Stay Healthy

To stay healthy, you can do the following practices: Eat well: Eat a variety of nutrient-dense foods, including fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean meats, eggs, nuts, and seeds. Adults should eat at least five portions of fruit and vegetables per day.
Exercise: Get at least 150 minutes of moderate activity a week. Exercise can help you maintain a healthy weight and reduce your risk of serious health conditions.
Sleep: Get 9 to 10 hours of sleep every night.
Limit alcohol: Limit your alcohol use.
Avoid tobacco: Avoid tobacco products, including cigarettes, e-cigarettes, or vapes. Get regular checkups: Visit the doctor regularly and get screened for cancers. Reduce screen time: Spend less time in front of a screen, like your cell phone, TV, or computer.
Drink fluids: Drink plenty of fluids, especially water, before, during, and after any exercise.
